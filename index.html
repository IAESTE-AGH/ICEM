Why should you join?

This August come to Cracow, the city of Kings! Here, you can wander throught the magical streets of Old Town and try to discover all of the mysteries of the city. Cracow have a lot to offer - astounding places, wonderful atmosphere, mighty dragon, and amazing nightclubs, full of life! To make your visit in Cracow unforgettable, our Local Committee will provide you with various attractions, such as: City game, sightseeing trips of best of the best parts of city, ending with all night long parties! Meet awesome people and make unforgettable memories!
